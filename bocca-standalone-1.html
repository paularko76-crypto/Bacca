Our Story

Where Italy meets
the heart of Rajasthan

Bocca brings the soul of Italian dining to Dungarpur — where every espresso is pulled with precision, every pizza stone-baked to perfection, and every dish tells a story of craft and passion.

We source the finest ingredients, from hand-selected Italian flour to locally grown herbs, blending tradition with the warmth and flavours of Rajasthan.

Whether you're here for a quiet morning cappuccino or a celebratory evening with family, Bocca is your table in Dungarpur.
